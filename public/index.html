About Our Project

Revolutionizing Heritage Exploration

Our project brings India's rich cultural heritage to life through the power of Augmented Reality. We've created an immersive experience that allows users to interact with 3D models of historical monuments right from their smartphones or computers.

Developed for the Smart India Hackathon, our solution aims to make learning about India's heritage more engaging and accessible to people of all ages.
